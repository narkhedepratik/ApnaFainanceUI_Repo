ELIGIBILITY AND DOCUMENTATION TO
APPLY FOR PERSONAL LOAN:


The Eligibility Criteria to apply for Personal Loan

  • Age: 21 years-55 years

  • Monthly Income: Minimum Rs.30,000

  • Interest Rate: 10.25% Uto 50 lakh

  • CIBIL Requirement: 700 or higher

  • Tenure: Between 1 month - 84 mmonths

Documents Required to apply for Personal Loan

  • Aadhar Card

  • PAN Card

  • Bank Statement of previous 6 months

  • Two latest salary slips/current dated salary certificates with hte latest Form 16
